q1 is: Answer(x,y) :- E(x,y),E(y,z),E(z,y),E(y,w)
q2 is: Answer(y,z) :- E(y,z),E(z,z),E(z,w)
A possible counterexample database D contains the following atoms:
E('x','y')
E('y','z')
E('z','y')
E('y','w')
Then q1(D) contains the tuple ('x','x')
However, ('x','x') is not in q2(D) since q2(D) is empty.
